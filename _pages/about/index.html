I am a Computer Science MSc student at EPFL, and currently a research intern at CEA in Paris, where I focus on scalable time series similarity search for seismic data collections.

Before EPFL, I obtained my BSc from the Computer Science Department, University of Crete, where I graduated first in class in July 2022.

Since 2021, I am collaborating with Dino Group, Universite Paris Cite and professors Panagiota Fatourou and Themis Palpanas, working on topics of distributed data series similarity search.

During the summer of 2022, I joined DIAS lab of EPFL, where I collaborated with professor Anastasia Ailamaki and Viktor Sanca to create an enhanced relational string-join operator using deep learning word embeddings.

During my early days in University of Crete, I was a research assistant at FORTH-ICS working with professor Yannis Tzitzikas and Dr. Michalis Mountantonakis in topics of Open Linked Data and Knowledge Graphs.

Throughout my studies I have received several awards, including undergraduate and graduate scholarships from University of Crete, Latsis Foundation, and Onassis Foundation, while I was also selected to participate as a young scientist in the 9th HLF.
